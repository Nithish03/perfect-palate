Welcome Chef

Browse through the categories of recipe shared by other chef's around the world.

Indian

Indian cuisine has intoxicating aromas, fresh blend of spices and rich ingredients that make it unique and special.The appeal of Indian cuisine is not just limited to Indians. Millions of people across the globe love it.

Continental

Continental cuisine comprises the cuisines of Europe including the cuisines brought to other countries by European settlers and colonists. Ingredients include mainly cheese, thyme, rosemery, oregano and most important olive oil.

Korean

Korean food is mostly made up of rice, noodles, vegetables, and meats. along with their steam-cooked rice. Sesame oil, soy sauce, salt, garlic, ginger and pepper are ingredients that are often used in the food.

Chinese

This food comes from many different parts of China and also the food made by Overseas Chinese that live in other parts of the world.  Important parts of Chinese food are rice, soy sauce, noodles, tea, and tofu.

Thai

Thai cuisine was influenced by Indian cuisine. Thai people learned how to use spices in their food in various ways from Indians. Today Thai food is its own, with a special unique blend of the 5 tastes: sweet, sour, bitter, salty, and spicy.

Caribbean

Caribbean cuisine is a fusion of African, Creole, Cajun, Amerindian, European, Latin American, Indian/South Asian, Middle Eastern, and Chinese. Ingredients may include garlic, onions, scotch bonnet peppers, celery, green onions
